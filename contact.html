Contact

Cervical Curve Guide is a static educational website. It does not currently provide clinical consultations, individualized exercise plans, diagnosis, or emergency support.

Corrections and site issues

For source corrections, broken links, translation issues, or site bugs, use the public repository issue tracker: GitHub issues .

Do not send medical information

Do not share personal medical history, imaging files, symptoms, medication lists, addresses, or other private health information through public issues or any static site channel.

Urgent symptoms

New weakness, spreading numbness, hand clumsiness, walking or balance changes, bowel or bladder symptoms, fever, cancer history, major trauma, or rapidly worsening symptoms should be handled through qualified medical care, not this website.
