# AdSense Readiness Notes

Last updated: 2026-06-09

## Implemented

- HTTPS canonical URLs and sitemap submission.
- Privacy, terms, disclaimer, editorial policy, medical review policy, contact, authors.
- Visible advertising placeholders that do not block content.
- Source-backed health education with conservative wording and red-flag notices.
- Multilingual content clusters and internal links.
- GA4 with consent-aware analytics.

## Before Applying

- Keep adding original, useful articles until the site has stable impressions and enough depth across clusters.
- Add `ads.txt` only after a real publisher ID is available.
- Avoid sticky or interstitial ads on medical content until approval is stable.
- Keep YouTube videos as references, not thin replacement content.
- Keep medical claims conservative: no guaranteed curve restoration, no diagnosis by finger map, no promised treatment outcome.

## Suggested Initial Ad Layout

- One in-content ad after the first substantial section.
- One ad near the end of long articles.
- Optional desktop side or wide ad only where it does not distract from medical guidance.
- Avoid ads inside red-flag, disclaimer, or emergency-care sections.
