Privacy Policy

Cervical Curve Guide is a static educational website. We do not operate user accounts, contact forms, newsletters, payment forms, or advertising trackers in this version of the site.

Information stored on your device

The site stores your selected language in your browser's local storage so the language switcher can remember your preference. This value stays on your device unless you clear your browser data.

Server logs

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard server log information such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, and timestamp according to GitHub's own policies.

Third-party embedded content

Some pages show YouTube thumbnail images and link to third-party rehabilitation videos and resources, including YouTube and external clinical or educational websites. Those services may collect data according to their own privacy policies when thumbnails load or when you open their links.

Advertising and analytics

The site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate traffic, page engagement, scroll depth, and outbound link clicks so the educational content can be improved. Google Analytics is loaded only after a visitor accepts the analytics notice shown on the site. Visitors may decline analytics in that notice.

Advertising code is not currently enabled. The Google Analytics configuration disables Google signals and ad personalization signals. If advertising is added later, this policy should be updated before launch to describe advertising partners, cookies, consent choices where required, and links to the relevant partner policies.

Health information

Do not send personal medical information through this static site. It is not designed to receive, store, or secure protected health information.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the site adds analytics, forms, newsletters, or other features. Material changes should be reflected by updating the date on this page.
